HIGH EFFICIENCY MOTORS WITH FOLDABLE PROPELLERS

ANAFI’s motors work in tandem to create an advanced propulsion system that lets the drone reach impressive speeds of up to 33pmh, and resist strong winds of up to 31pmh.

SMART BATTERY

ANAFI boasts a smart battery that offers a 25 minutes flight time. The smart battery also enables the use of Power Banks (up to 24W), charging up to 60% faster than standard chargers.

GPS AND GLONASS GEOPOSITIONING SYSTEM

ANAFI is capable of locating itself thanks to its integrated GPS and GLONASS positioning systems. This enables the use of the Smart Return To Home feature.

21MP CAMERA & 180° TILT GIMBAL

ANAFI features a 1/2.4” CMOS Sony sensor. The camera shoots 21MP photos and 4K HDR videos. The 180° tilt gimbal lets you shoot images from new perspectives such as low-angle shots and impressive aerial views.

COMPUTING SYSTEM

ANAFI is powered by a quad-core Ambarella image signal processor. All images are processed by a dedicated ISP, letting you create slow-motion and hyperlapse videos. ANAFI also includes vision positioning sensors and microSD card slot.

CARBON FRAME WITH FOLDABLE LEGS AND DUAL-BAND ANTENNAS

ANAFI's frame is reinforced by carbon fibers charged with empty glass microbeads. The patented double-hinge system lets ANAFI unfold in less that 3 seconds. Each of the drone’s legs contains a dual-band antenna that extends the flight range up to 2.5mi.
